About me

Hi! My name is Yolan Ibnu Prasetya, a System Analyst specializing in Information Systems. I graduated from the Information Systems department at Universitas Bakrie and currently live in Bekasi.

Throughout my academic and professional journey, I have developed a passion for designing, analyzing, and optimizing information systems that help organizations achieve their goals efficiently. I am particularly interested in how technology can improve business processes and support data-driven decision making.

With several years of experience in system analysis, I continuously strive to stay updated with the latest technology trends to ensure the solutions I work on are innovative and effective. My expertise includes system development life cycle, data modeling, and IT project management.

I am committed to applying my knowledge and skills to solve complex problems and contribute to the growth and success of the organizations I collaborate with.
